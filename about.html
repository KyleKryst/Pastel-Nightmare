Who Is Pastel Nightmare?

The story behind the clothing company

Origins

Who are we? Pastel Nightmare clothing company was created in late 2023 and strives to give people alternative clothing to make them look and feel more comfortable while expressing their creativity and personality through their designs and colors. The biggest design challenge we've had in the past years was trying to come up with different ideas that would suit the needs of our customers as well as inspire our employees and staff to create beautifully creepy products.

Now that we are up and running, customers are now free to express their personality through our uniquely creepy apparel.
